Software Quality Assurance (SQA)

Full time On Site 2-3 years experiences

Hybritech Innovations Ltd. is a dynamic and innovative Software company that is committed to delivering high-quality solutions to our clients. As an SQA Engineer at Hybritech Innovations Ltd. you will play a critical role in ensuring the quality and reliability of our software products. You’ll collaborate closely with our development team to define, develop, and execute test strategies. Your work will directly contribute to the success of our products by helping us deliver flawless experiences to our customers.

Responsibilities:

1. Design, develop, and execute test cases, scripts, plans, and procedures to identify and resolve product defects.

2. Perform functional, regression, load, and performance testing to ensure software meets all requirements and standards.

3. Collaborate with developers and project managers to troubleshoot issues, resolve bugs, and ensure timely releases.

4. Identify test automation opportunities and contribute to the development of automated testing frameworks.

5. Track and report testing progress, issues, and metrics; provide clear and actionable feedback to the development team.

6. Participate in code reviews, sprint planning, and release cycles to align testing strategies with development goals.

7. Continuously research and recommend tools, processes, and practices to enhance SQA effectiveness and efficiency.

Requirements:

1. Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field

2. 2-3 years of experience in software quality assurance, testing, or a related field.

3. Strong understanding of software QA methodologies, tools, and processes.

4. Experience with automated testing tools (e.g., Selenium, JUnit, TestNG) is a plus.

5. Knowledge of Agile methodologies and experience working in an Agile/Scrum environment.

6. Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

Salary: Salary Based on Skills
